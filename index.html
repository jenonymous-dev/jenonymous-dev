Welcome to Taste the Rainbow, the premier recipe site for all your vibrant and colourful culinary needs! Here you will find a selection of recipes to cook up a rainbow-themed feast.

What is the Rainbow?

The rainbow is a beautiful natural phenomenon consisting of seven distinct colours - red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo and violet - arranged in a colourful arc. Each colour has its own unique flavour and texture.
