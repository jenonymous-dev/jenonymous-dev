GaGa Loves You: Near & Far

GaGa

A little poem for my Layla, to let her know, GaGa is always thinking about her. ü©∑

GaGa loves you long distance, across all these miles. That's why, when I see you, I instantly smile.
I replay fun thoughts when I start to miss you. Daydream about next visits, and what we'll get into.
On clear, starry nights I glance up at the moon. You can see the same one, and we'll play someday soon.
We can push shoes in a stroller, and always say, "No!" Or stay up past bedtime, Mommy doesn't have to know.
No matter the distance, our love can't be broken. Just open your heart, and Gaga's love will flow in.
